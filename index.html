Who Are We?

At DOREP, we believe that a little support can change the world. Since our founding in 2007, we have focused on making a meaningful impact through fresh ideas and passion in our various activities. Our mission is to empower the destitute by launching community projects that help our members utilize available resources for poverty eradication..

Dorep at a glance

DOREP is a Non-Governmental Organization that has been established to assist people in alleviating poverty through empowerment and capacity building. We give start-up capital to people who approach us in groups for the purposes of establishing projects. Some of the projects that our members are involved in include:

Dairy and poultry farming

Rabbit and bee keeping

Greenhouse farming

Training and technical advisory

Our Activities include:

Seminars & Workshops

seminars, workshops, and conferences pertaining to health, education, water, religion, and agricultural needs.

Fundraising

Raising, mobilizing, and disbursing funds and other resources for the promotion of the organization.

Employing advisors, counselors, experts, and other staff for all or any of the objectives/mission of the organization.

Community Empowerment Activities:

Guidance & Counselling Department

We provide wheelchairs to the physically challenged.We are also involved in the fight against drug abuse and excessive drinking through counseling and rehabilitation services.We offer support to people infected and affected by HIV & AIDS.

Children Home Outreach

We organise occasional visits to children's homes around Kenya and we get to meet new people and we are able to donate foodstuffs and clothes to the children and the organisations that run those departments.

GreenHouse Farming

DOREP constructs greenhouses to members and encourages them to construct a structure with a glass or plastic roof and walls to create an artificial climate for maximum yields. The management and supply of seedlings such as flowers, vegetables, fruits and tobacco is undertaken by the organization.
